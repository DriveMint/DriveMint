Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to rent a car?
You need a valid driver's license, Aadhar card for identity proof, and a refundable security deposit. For self-drive rentals, your license should be at least 1 year old.
Is there a minimum rental period?
Our minimum rental period is 24 hours. However, we offer flexible packages for hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly rentals based on your requirements.
Are the vehicles insured?
Yes, all our vehicles come with comprehensive insurance coverage. However, the driver is responsible for any damages caused due to negligence or violation of traffic rules.
Can I take the car outside Trichy?
Yes, we allow outstation trips. Please inform us in advance about your travel plans so we can provide you with the best package and ensure proper documentation.
What is your fuel policy?
We follow a full-to-full fuel policy. The vehicle is provided with a full tank, and you're expected to return it with a full tank. Alternatively, fuel charges will be deducted from your deposit.
Do you provide driver services?
Yes, we have experienced and professional drivers available on request. Driver charges are additional and vary based on the duration and distance of your trip.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations made 24 hours before the pickup time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a cancellation fee. Please contact us for specific terms.
Are there any mileage restrictions?
Our standard packages come with unlimited kilometers for local trips. For outstation journeys, specific mileage limits may apply depending on the package you choose. Additional kilometers are charged nominally.
